Principles

How projects are shaped.

A small set of principles keeps each product recognisable while allowing it to develop its own purpose and identity.

01 · SIMPLE

Clear by default

Interfaces should reveal what matters and remove unnecessary visual or cognitive noise.

02 · ADAPTIVE

Natural everywhere

Software should feel intentional across phones, tablets, foldables and larger displays.

03 · OPEN

Built to be learned from

Projects can document their decisions, share progress and encourage constructive improvement.
