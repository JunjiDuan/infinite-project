Privacy

Your browsing stays yours.

Infinite Voyager is designed to provide a private, user-controlled browsing experience.

Information collection

Infinite Voyager does not require an account and does not intentionally collect, sell or share personal information.

Browsing data

History, bookmarks and preferences remain on your device unless you choose to sync or back them up using other services.

Device permissions

The app may request access needed to open links, files or sites you choose. Permissions are used only to provide browsing features.

Changes

This notice may be updated as Infinite Voyager develops. Material changes will be documented in the project repository.
